About Smart Structure

30+ Years of Excellence in Structural Engineering

Our Founder

Eng. Ahmed Abdel Malek El Maghraby

Smart Structure was founded by Eng. Ahmed Abdel Malek El Maghraby to share his extensive experience and serve professionally the purpose of free span and hi-tech structures. Previously owning "Space Builders & Designers" in Egypt since 1995, Eng. Ahmed has built strong professional relationships with international suppliers worldwide.

With more than 30 years of experience in the field and hundreds of projects, Eng. Ahmed is a pioneer of special free span structures in the Middle East.

Well established international practice covering architecture, planning and industrial design in all major sectors, we breathe life into buildings through beautiful form and function.
