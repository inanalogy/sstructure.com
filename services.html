Specialized Solutions

Space Frame Structures

Advanced space frame systems offering large free span coverage with architectural flexibility and aesthetic appeal. Ideal for airports, sports halls, and exhibition centers.

  • Large free span coverage
  • High structural efficiency
  • Quick installation
  • 25+ years durability

Tensile Fabric Structures

Innovative tensile membrane structures providing unique architectural forms with excellent weather protection. Perfect for canopies, stadiums, and outdoor spaces.

  • Lightweight construction
  • Natural light transmission
  • Weather resistant
  • 10-15 years lifespan

Skylights

Energy-efficient skylight systems with aluminum and steel profiles. Enhance natural lighting while providing excellent thermal performance and weather protection.

  • Natural daylight
  • Energy savings
  • UV protection
  • Low maintenance
